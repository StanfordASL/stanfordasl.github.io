Xilun Zhang is a PhD student at Stanford University, advised by Prof. Marco Pavone in the Autonomous Systems Lab. Before joining Stanford, he received his M.S. from Carnegie Mellon University. His research focuses on developing generalist robot policies, with a particular interest in test-time adaptation and action representations. Outside of research, Xilun enjoys playing sports such as badminton and ping pong.
